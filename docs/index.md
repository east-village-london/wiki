# East Village London Wiki

Welcome to the East Village London Wiki - a community-maintained knowledge base for residents of [East Village, London (wikipedia)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/East_Village,_London).

## What's in this wiki

This wiki is written by residents, for residents. It covers practical topics that are hard to find answers to elsewhere - from maintaining your home to navigating local services.

### Resident Handbook

Practical guides for keeping your home in good shape, written with East Village's specific fixtures and fittings in mind.

- [Resident Handbook](resident-handbook/index.md)

## Contributing

If you know something useful that isn't here, add it. Keep entries factual, specific to East Village London where relevant, and written for someone encountering the problem for the first time.
