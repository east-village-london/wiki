# Resident Handbook

Practical guides for East Village residents covering the most common maintenance tasks and questions about the estate's fixtures, fittings, and systems.

East Village homes use a consistent set of materials and systems across the development, so advice that works for one flat or house is usually applicable across the estate.

## General tips

- Before doing any work that affects building structure, services, or external appearance, check your tenancy or lease agreement and contact Triathlon Homes or Get Living.
